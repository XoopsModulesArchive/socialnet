::TRANSLATION AND CORRECTIONS WITH THE SOCIALNET TRANSLATION TOOL::

1. Look to the left side in the user's block, language tool.
YOURSITE/modules/socialnet/langbegin.php

Instructions: Select socialnet module, 
click send button, and follow the instructions of the language tool program.

2. If your language doesn't appear in the translation tools section, 
create it through the socialnet module administrator.

::IMPORTANT::
Once you have accomplished the corrections, please send the files to support@ipwgc.com
in order to update the language folders in different languages.
